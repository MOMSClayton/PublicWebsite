Who We Are

The MOMS Club® of Clayton, NC is a chapter of the International MOMS Club® serving the Clayton area of North Carolina. We are a fun support group dedicated to helping mothers and their children. We have a variety of scheduled group activities for moms, kids and families like mom's night out, organized play dates, book club, dinner nights and much more! We are also a non-profit, service organization, so we work together on a variety of fundraising and service projects throughout the year that benefit our community needs.

A notice on COVID19

We ARE still active and are still working to support moms and their kids in any way possible. Due to COVID19 we are limiting the type and number of playdates occurring each month. However we are still keeping everyone active via Facebook and Zoom meetings.
